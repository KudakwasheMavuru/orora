About Us

Since 2022

ORORA Investment Group (“ORORA”) was launched in 2022 to develop and enhance productivity in the livestock supply chain through successful investments and professional business management. ORORA is an operator, advisor and investor offering true alignment with our customers. ORORA brings a global perspective to clients utilising a commercial approach with vast agribusiness and geographical experience.
